1. The Review Reality: AI vs. Humans

Why do your ads get rejected instantly? Because humans aren't reading them. Meta's review system is primarily automated using advanced AI (PyTorch). It processes millions of ads daily, looking for patterns linked to financial fraud and poor user experience.

Key Insight:

  • AI rejections happen in milliseconds.
  • Manual review is only triggered for appeals or high-spend accounts.
  • Compliance is about "teaching" the AI that you are safe.

2. Inside the "Black Box": Meta's Detection Engine

Meta's AI doesn't just read text. It "sees" your images and "hears" your video audio. It uses Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to read text on images and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand intent. Below is the sensitivity level of different AI detection modules for Finance ads.

Pattern Matching

The AI compares your creative against a database of banned ads. If your image looks like a scam (e.g., piles of cash, rising green arrows), it gets flagged immediately.

OCR & Audio Scan

Text overlays on images are read as plain text. Even if you don't write "ROI" in the caption, if it's on the image, you get banned. Audio in videos is transcribed and analyzed.

Contextual Analysis

The AI looks at the relationship between your Ad, your Landing Page, and your Disclaimer. A mismatch triggers a "Circumventing Systems" ban.
