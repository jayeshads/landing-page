Audience Strategy

Targeting that Silently Kills Ads

Targeting "Binary Options" or specific "Crypto Coins" is a trap. These interests are flagged. When you combine a risky creative with a risky interest, your ban probability hits 100%.

❌ Avoid High-Risk Interests

  • Specific Crypto Coins (Bitcoin, Ethereum)
  • "Binary Options"
  • "Passive Income"
  • "Get Rich" Keywords

✅ Use Safe "Affinity" Interests

  • Luxury Lifestyle / Luxury Goods
  • Business Travel / First Class
  • Financial News (Bloomberg, Forbes)
  • Entrepreneurship / Small Business

Pro Tip: The Location Trap

Avoid targeting Tier 3 countries known for click farms alongside Tier 1 countries in the same ad set. It confuses the algorithm and can flag your account for "Circumventing Systems". Stick to one economic tier per campaign.
