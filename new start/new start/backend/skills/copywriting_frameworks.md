# High-Converting Ad Copywriting Frameworks

## 1. PAS Framework (Problem - Agitate - Solution)
- **Problem**: Highlight a common frustration your target customer faces.
- **Agitate**: Emphasize why ignoring this issue causes further loss or headache.
- **Solution**: Present your product/service as the ultimate hassle-free solution.

## 2. AIDA Framework (Attention - Interest - Desire - Action)
- **Attention**: Catchy hook or pattern interrupt in the first 5 words.
- **Interest**: Relatable benefit or intriguing feature.
- **Desire**: Social proof, discount, or emotional transformation.
- **Action**: Clear Call-to-Action (CTA) like "Shop Now", "Claim 20% OFF", "Book Free Demo".

## 3. Direct Offer Framework (Best for Indian D2C & Lead Gen)
- Clear value proposition + Discount Code + Urgency + Risk Reversal (COD available / Free shipping / Money back guarantee).
