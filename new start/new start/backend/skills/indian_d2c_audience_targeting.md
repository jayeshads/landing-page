# Indian D2C & Lead Generation Audience Targeting Matrix

## Tier 1 & Metro Audiences
- Demographics: Age 22-45, Metro & Tier 1 Cities (Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai).
- High Intent Interests: Online Shopping, E-commerce, Premium Brands, Digital Payments (UPI / Credit Card).

## Category-Specific Interest Bundles
1. **Home & Living / Decor**: Home Decor, Interior Design, Scented Candles, Aromatherapy, Housewarming.
2. **Fashion & Apparel**: Online Shopping, Fashion Accessories, Sustainable Fashion, Luxury Goods.
3. **Health & Wellness**: Organic Products, Fitness, Yoga, Skincare, Personal Care.
4. **B2B / Lead Gen**: Business Owners, Entrepreneurship, Small Business Administration, Marketing.
